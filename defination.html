BIG DATA: Big data refers to the large, complex knowledge sets which are rising at ever-increasing levels. It encompasses the volume of information, the speed or speed at which it is created and gathered, and the variety or scope of the data points covered. Big data often originates from multiple sources, arriving in multiple formats.One may categorize big data as unstructured or organized. Structured data is composed of information already managed in databases and spreadsheets by the organization; it is often numerical in nature. Unstructured data is unorganized information that doesn't fall into a fixed pattern or format. It covers data collected from social media sources,Which helps institutions gather customer requirement information.
